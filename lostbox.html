Lost Box

It is the day of the final exam and three teams are starting their journey to find their professors ‘lost box’ and return it to him in the hopes of earning their adventuring licence. A licence you need to adventure professionally. Failing the test means becoming a monster, the nemesis of the adventurer. Students get three chances to pass the exam, if they fail, it’s game over. Team up in groups of four, use teamwork to navigate through a dungeon maze, battling monsters and defeating bosses all to find your professor's lost box. Play as one of four hero characters, each with their own unique play style.

For this Unreal Engine 4 project my class of about 30 students split into smaller groups for Core, AI, Mechanics, UI and Sound. I was the team lead in charge of making AI my task to make sure everyone had what i assigned to them done on schedule and also make the Ally AI that follow the player around and help him on his test.
