4v4 Arena

4v4 Arena is a remake of our failed class project TheSpot where my group was supposed to make a game called BloodSport to go into it. 4v4 Arena is currently in development for me and my friends studio Devoture Studio.

4v4 Arena is made in Unity and will be a 4v4 networked game where you have 4 classes Mage, Warrior, Hunter, Rogue. All classes have unique abilities, last team standing wins.
